About Us

Namba Coffee Estate is a premier Kenyan coffee estate dedicated to cultivating high-quality Arabica coffee beans. Our farm benefits from Kenya's high-altitude climate and unique wet-processing methods, resulting in bright, complex flavors with citrus and berry notes. We are committed to sustainable farming practices and fostering community growth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce world-class coffee while supporting sustainable agriculture and improving the lives of our local farmers and community.
